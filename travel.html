VISA?
Most EU countries including the Netherlands and Poland do not require any visas, until 2024, for 3 months. For more informations, visit official website.
Mobile data?
You can buy SIM card for your phone with unlimited internet OR pocket WIFI (a portable wifi router) for multiple devices at the airport. Multiple services are available for the pocket wifi like Klook and Dosirak where you can also reserve online.
Transportation?
Buy a T-Money card at the airport, located near the subway entrance (or at convinience stores later.) Card can be used in any public transports like buses and subways and sometimes in taxies. They can be charged at any subways stations or convinience stores after purchasing.
